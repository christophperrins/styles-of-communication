Communication Styles

There are four main communication styles each with their own set of pros and cons. By understanding which style you are more alligned with you are able to work out your place in a team

The four communication styles are:

  • Action - like to get things done, and tend to solve problems quickly
  • Process - like to organize
  • People - concerned with human relationships
  • Ideas - like concepts and new approaches

Self-Assessment Exercise

Select from each pair of attributes the one which is most typical of your personality. No pair is an either-or proposal. Make your choice as spontaneously as possible. There is no wrong answer.
